About

The honest version.

"My first business was a food stall at nineteen. I ran two at once for three years. That's where I learned to be comfortable with chaos."

— On where it started

I graduated from Universitas Padjadjaran in November 2025 with a degree in Agroindustrial Technology — not Computer Science, not Marketing — and a 3.84 GPA. I got into performance marketing and AI automation through curiosity and necessity, not a prescribed path. That tends to produce people who actually understand how things work underneath.

On the marketing side, I run Meta Ads with a structured TOFU-MOFU-BOFU framework — testing creatives systematically, reading the numbers, iterating. My best campaign hit a 2.91× ROAS and a single Before-After ad reached 7.6×. I also grew a client's monthly revenue fourfold through B2B partnership development, closing deals with BNI Sekuritas, Maybank, Astra Infra, TEDxBandung, and ten-plus universities through pure outreach — no budget.

On the systems side, I have built three AI automation tools end-to-end: a pipeline that pulls from Meta, Google, and LinkedIn Ads APIs and writes reports via Telegram; a WhatsApp sales agent with five tool-calls; and a RAG knowledge assistant with vector search. Not tutorials — real builds wiring live APIs into working flows.

I'm currently completing the CFEL certification in E-Commerce & Digital Commerce with Life At FMCG × Luhung Unpad. The goal is simple: be the rare marketer who can both run the campaign and build the machine that scales it.
